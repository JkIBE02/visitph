Palawan

Puerto Princesa, the capital of Palawan, is a city known for its lush greenery, crystal-clear waters, and dedication to environmental preservation. Dubbed the "City in a Forest," it is home to stunning natural wonders like the UNESCO-listed Underground River, where visitors can explore limestone caves by boat.
